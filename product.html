Our Product

Semi Husked Coconut

Grade High Quality fresh, diameter start 8-14 cm for the weight/nut 4gram skinless- 1200gram with or without skinless.

Young Coconut

Fresh coconut with water and fruit flesh that is very beneficial for the body, one of which is heart disease, maintaining healthy skin, increasing body immunity. by consuming it regularly

Copra

Copra is the dried flesh of the coconut. Copra is one of the most important coconut derivative products, because it is the raw material for making coconut oil and its derivatives.

Coconut Fiber

Contains an acidity level of PH 5.5-6.5, good water absorption and is able to store water reserves for plants. Useful to keep loose, fertile and make it easier for new roots to grow quickly and densely.

Cocopeat

Cocopeat is a medium that has a fairly high water holding capacity, reaching 14.71 times its dry weight. cocopeat has water content and water storage capacity of 119% and 695.4%, respectively

Briquette

A block charcoal made from coconut shell charcoal. The heat generated is very hot. The briquette that we have is made from shell charcoal which is pulverized and then molded as needed and is usually used for bbq, sisha and other household needs

Brown Sugar

Brown sugar is made from palm sap or juice from coconut trees. By boiling this brown sugar is made. The boiling process is carried out long enough to make the coconut tree juice into a caramel which will later be molded like brown sugar found in the market.

Cooking Oil

Coconut oil is a vegetable oil extracted from coconut meat. Based on the extraction technique, coconut oil can be classified into three types: Virgin Coconut Oil, Coconut Oil Non-RBD and RBD Coconut Oil.

Virgin Oil

Coconut oil obtained by extraction or compression at a temperature of no more than 60 °Celsius, so that the resulting oil is clear like water and the nutritional content, aroma and taste of coconut are well preserved.

Broom Stick

As a yard cleaning tool, the yard comes from the leaves of the coconut tree. Sticks from oil palm trees are stronger and of good quality, and can even be modified into handicrafts.
